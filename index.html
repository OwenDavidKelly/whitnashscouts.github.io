Welcome to
1st Whitnash Scouts

We are a Scout Group based in Whitnash, we believe in preparing young people with skills for life. We encourage young people to do more, learn more and be more. Each week, we help over 40 young people aged 6-25 enjoy fun and adventure while developing the skills they need to succeed, now and in the future.
